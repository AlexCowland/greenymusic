Bio

Greeny and the Voyagers is fronted by singer-songwriter Greeny hailing from Nelson, England. After years performing around the world with many talented musicians Greeny has decided to strike out on his own and bring his music to the masses.

Well crafted and consistent from beginning to end in terms of songwriting and production, Greeny's music forays into elements of shoegaze, acoustic rock and a nod to mainstream guitar pop.

Greeny and the Voyagers' first solo EP, 'Incident of the Letter', is now set for release.
